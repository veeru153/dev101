Programming

Most of this demo happens in the console. To open the console, you can either:

- Press Ctrl+Shift+J or Cmd+Option+J

- Right-click to open the menu and press the "Inspect" or similar option and choose the "Console". If you do follow this approach, click on the tiny square on the top-left part of the new pane to stop inspecting elements.

If you see "Hello World!" written in the console, you are good to go! Return to the series and follow the steps.
